Integrated Treatment Process
Primary treatment provides initial separation and conditioning to remove free oil and contaminants. High-efficiency oil skimmers maximize hydrocarbon recovery. Produced water then progresses through multi-stage filtration utilizing oil filtration units, sand filters, and cartridge filtration systems to reduce Total Suspended Solids (TSS) and residual hydrocarbons. Clean water is then stored and reinjected through integrated pumping solutions.
Engineered for Performance. Designed for Sustainability.
Fully modular, skid-mounted, and pre-engineered, each system is designed for rapid mobilization, streamlined installation, and accelerated project execution.
